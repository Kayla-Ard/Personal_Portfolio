About Me

My Background

I am originally from Louisiana. I grew up in a home with my mother and brother. I moved away when I graduated from High School to take advantage of a college basketball scholarship I earned. After graduating from college I worked for Anheuser-Busch for a few years in marketing and sales before pursuing my dream of becoming a college basketball coach.
